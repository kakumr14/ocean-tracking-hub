🌍 Incoterms Guide

Quick reference guide for commonly used Incoterms.

FOB

Free On Board.
Seller loads cargo onboard vessel. Buyer arranges freight.

CIF

Cost Insurance Freight.
Seller pays freight and insurance.

EXW

Ex Works.
Buyer takes responsibility from seller's premises.

DDP

Delivered Duty Paid.
Seller handles nearly everything.

FCA

Free Carrier.
Seller delivers cargo to nominated carrier.

DAP

Delivered At Place.
Seller delivers to destination.

FOB vs CIF

Feature FOB CIF
Ocean Freight Buyer Seller
Insurance Buyer Seller
Export Clearance Seller Seller
Import Clearance Buyer Buyer
Risk Transfer On Board Vessel On Board Vessel

EXW vs FCA

Feature EXW FCA
Loading Buyer Seller
Export Clearance Buyer Seller
Responsibility Maximum Buyer Shared

DAP vs DDP

Feature DAP DDP
Import Duty Buyer Seller
Import Tax Buyer Seller
Final Delivery Seller Seller

When Should You Use Each Incoterm?

FOB
✔ Buyer wants control of freight.
✔ Buyer has preferred freight forwarder.

CIF
✔ Seller arranges shipment.
✔ Simpler buying process.

EXW
✔ Experienced importer.
✔ Strong logistics network at origin.

DDP
✔ Door delivery required.
✔ Buyer wants minimum logistics involvement.
